TOKYO DRIFT

Artist

  • Singer:- Teriyaki Boyz
  • Lyrics:- J Hip Hop
  • Producer:- The Neptunes
  • Origin:- Tokyo, Japan(2005)

Details

“Tokyo Drift”, a single performed by the Japanese hip hop group Teriyaki Boyz, is a track featured in the 2006 movie The Fast and the Furious:
Tokyo Drift as the main theme. Produced by the Neptunes (Pharrell Williams and Chad Hugo), the song is described by critics to be the Teriyaki Boyz’s most popular song
